Every asbestos survey we deliver aligns to the current Victorian regulatory stack.

  • OHS Regulations 2017 (Vic) — Part 4.4
  • Compliance Code: Managing Asbestos (WorkSafe Vic)

§ 01 — Process

Five stages, one defensible outcome.

01
Brief & scope
Site walkthrough with the facility owner or PC. We identify buildings, ages, previous reports, occupation windows, and known refurbishment plans. Written scope back within 48 hours.
02
On-site survey
Licensed Asbestos Assessor on site. Room-by-room inventory. Non-destructive Division 5 or intrusive Division 6 depending on scope. Photograph, geo-tag, and physically flag each suspect material.
03
NATA lab analysis
Samples chain-of-custody to our accredited lab. Polarised Light Microscopy per AS 4964:2004. Turnaround 3-5 business days standard; 24-hour available.
04
Report & register
Full audit report + asbestos register in the exact format WorkSafe inspectors expect. Risk-rated (low/med/high), condition-scored, removal-prioritised. Digital register handover.
05
Air monitoring & clearance
If removal follows: control monitoring during works, clearance inspection, clearance certificate. Sign-off you can hand to the incoming tenant, the builder, or the sale contract.

§ 04 — Common questions

What facility managers ask us first.

How is your report different from the one I got five years ago? +
Most legacy registers list "presumed asbestos" without sampling and don't align to the 2017 OHS Regs register format. We sample, we lab-test, we risk-rate, and we deliver a register your incoming builder or WorkSafe inspector can actually work from.
Can you turn around a survey in one week? +
Typical residential or single-tenancy commercial: yes. Complex multi-building sites: usually two to three weeks including lab. Rush turnaround available for settlement or transaction deadlines.
Do you also do the removal? +
Deliberately no. We are the independent assessor. Removal is a licensed activity we don't compete in, which is precisely why our reports and clearance certificates aren't contested by removalists.
What does a survey cost? +
Priced by building floor area, complexity, and sample count — not a per-hour block. Typical small commercial (~500 m²): $2,400-$3,600 including lab. Written fixed-fee quote within 48 hours of scope call.
Will you attend site meetings with our builder? +
Yes. Included in scope for jobs over $8k; hourly rate for smaller engagements. Common for pre-start and clearance meetings on refurbishment sites.
